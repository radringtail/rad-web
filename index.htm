Raised for community-representing charities

Fundraising has been an important part of my stream. Being somewhat turned off by the self-gain nature of streaming, I've sought to make sure we are regularly holding charity marathons and events.

While employed, I have also ensured that all funding raised by streaming has either gone back into purchases for the stream (artwork, equipment, and software licenses) or been donated to charity via support other fundraising events.

The charities I have chosen to support represent my, and my community's, own experiences: queer, furry, neurodivergence, and non-traditional families.
